Dear Father Christmas...

2015 has been awesome…

I hope this letter finds you well and that the weather is good where you are.

It is snowing here but my festive jumper and ceremonial robes help me ignore that.

The farmer plays music in the cow shed next door, and he's streaming Jona Lewie and The Pogues, so Christmas is definitely here, and so here is my list...

I would (please) like...

  • Sugar Lumps
  • Some Hay
  • A William Blake poster for my crib.
  • Packet of Golden Virginia
    I like the smell of this, but never site fire to it - that would be too dangerous with all the hay.
  • A monocle

Yours sincerely

G G Horse PhD
